UI / GRAPHIC DESIGN

Godfather Typography

I wanted to play around with type and shape. In Adobe Illustrator I used the envelope distort tool in the object tab to lay the text into the shape I wanted. Then for each letter I moved all the anchor points to the desired area to still maintain the shapes outline. Finally, I put different shades of red to make each word easier to read.
